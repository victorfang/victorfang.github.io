The Matthew Effect Explained

The Matthew Effect, derived from the Gospel of Matthew, has become increasingly apparent in the AI industry. The Magnificent 7 companies have leveraged their existing advantages:

  • Vast data resources
  • Established cloud infrastructure
  • Deep talent pools
  • Strong cash positions
  • Existing customer relationships

Conclusion

As we progress through 2025, the AI market continues to demonstrate the Matthew Effect in action. The Magnificent 7's dominance raises important questions about market concentration and innovation in the AI sector.
